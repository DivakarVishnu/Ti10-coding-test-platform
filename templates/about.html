⚓ About Titan

Every student who logs in sees the same small ship crossing a quiet purple sea — that's Titan, Ti10's mascot. The idea is simple: a coding test can feel like open water — a little uncertain, sometimes a little scary — but with the right vessel, you make it across. Titan sails past the same moon and stars on every page, a quiet reminder that the platform is steady even when the exam itself is intense.

The name "Ti10" nods to that same idea — small, sturdy, and built to carry a class safely through a timed test without drama.
